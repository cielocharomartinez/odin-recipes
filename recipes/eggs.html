Sunny side up eggs:

This recipe will teach you how to prepare sunny side up eggs

This is an easy and delicious recipe for you to prepare as a quick snack

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • oil of your preference
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • bread

Steps

  1. Heat up a frying pan until it is hot
  2. Add your preferred oil until it heats up but not burnt
  3. Carefully break the egg into the pan
  4. Cover with a lid and wait until the white is cooked, in the meantime put the bread in the toaster
  5. Serve the egg on top of the bread, add a dash of salt and pepper and enjoy
