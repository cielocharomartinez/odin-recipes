Gnocchi

Mozzarella-stuffed pumkin gnocchi:

This recipe will teach you how to prepare mozzarella-stuffed pumpkin gnocchi

This is an easy and delicious recipe for those cold winter nigths to prepare with your loved ones

Ingredients

  • 1 pumpkin
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups flour
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 500gr mozzarella

Steps

  1. Bake the sliced pumpkin in the oven until its soft, approx. 50 minutes
  2. smash it with a fork to make it puree, and add the egg and the flour
  3. Make them into little balls and add the mozzarella pieces inside
  4. Bring water to a boil and add the gnocchi
  5. Once they start floating they are done
